Overview

Shidori is a pioneering subscription-based food delivery app that delivers authentic, home-style regional Indian cuisine to users’ doorsteps on a daily basis. Unlike traditional on-demand food delivery services, Shidori focuses on recurring, customizable meal plans, enabling users to regularly enjoy flavours from any Indian state, whether they are living at home, in another city, or away for work or study.

Unique Selling Points (USPs)

  1. Consistent Tiffin Service

    A daily subscription-based tiffin-like service providing reliable, home-cooked-style meals. Users avoid the hassle of cooking every day or repeatedly ordering from restaurants.

  2. National Culinary Journey

    Users can explore traditional dishes from all over India, with menus curated by state/region, such as:

    • Maharashtra: thalis, vada pav, misal, poha, puran poli
    • Punjab: butter chicken, dal makhani, parathas
    • Bengal (incl. Kolkata): seafood, Bengali sweets, rice dishes
    • Hyderabad: iconic biryanis, kebabs, curries
    • South India (Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, etc.): dosas, idlis, uttapams, biryanis, spicy curries

  3. Customizable Meal Plans & Dietary Tracking

    Highly customizable meals with:

    • Portion sizes (standard or large)
    • Spice levels (mild, medium, spicy)
    • Dietary preferences (vegetarian, vegan, non-vegetarian)
    • Calorie-specific plans (keto, high-protein, low-carb, low-calorie, balanced diet)
    • Allergen filters (dairy-free, nut-free, gluten-free, etc.)
    • Each meal comes with detailed nutritional information (calories, proteins, fats, carbs, vitamins, minerals) and can be part of predefined diet plans.

Subscription & Ordering Options

  • One-Time Orders

    Ideal for sampling new cuisines or dishes without committing to a subscription.

  • Flexible Subscription Plans

    • Single meal per day: lunch or dinner
    • Two meals per day: lunch and dinner
    • Full-day: breakfast, lunch, and dinner

  • Pause/Resume Subscriptions

    Users can pause the service while travelling or during breaks and resume anytime, offering flexibility and cost efficiency.

  • Payment & Billing Plans

    • Weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, or pay-as-you-go
    • Easy auto-renewal and upgrade/downgrade options
    • No hidden fees for managing subscriptions

Cuisine & Menu Variety

  • State-Specific Menus

    Menus curated by region/state with authentic, home-style recipes, emphasising local taste and tradition.

  • Daily Menu Rotation

    Menus change regularly, allowing users to try new dishes every day to avoid monotony.

  • Home-Style Cooking

    Focus on comfort food that feels like a meal from home, emphasising freshness, quality, and familiar flavours rather than heavy restaurant-style cooking.

Advanced Customisation & Health Features

  • Portion sizes: standard/large

  • Spice levels: mild/medium/spicy

  • Dietary choices: vegetarian, vegan, non-vegetarian, calorie-specific

  • Allergen filters: dairy-free, nut-free, gluten-free, etc.

  • Detailed nutritional info for each meal

  • Predefined diet plans: balanced diet, high-protein, low-calorie, low-carb, keto-like patterns

The app can also provide diet recommendations and meal suggestions based on user preferences, history, and health goals, including automated calorie tracking across meals.

Order Tracking & Notifications

  • Real-Time Order Tracking

    Track the meal from kitchen to doorstep with live updates.

  • Timely Alerts

    Notifications for:

    • Meal preparation started
    • Order en route
    • Order delivered

  • Scheduled Delivery

    Users can choose preferred delivery time windows for breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

Hygiene, Safety & Sustainability

  • Stringent Hygiene Protocols

    Partner kitchens undergo regular inspections and must follow strict safety standards.

  • Temperature-Controlled Delivery

    Meals are packed in insulated containers to maintain optimal temperature and freshness.

  • Eco-Friendly Packaging

    Use of biodegradable and recyclable materials.

  • Opt-Out of Cutlery

    Users can choose not to receive disposable cutlery to reduce waste.

  • Quality Assurance

    Regular quality checks and audits to ensure only the best meals reach the customer.

  • Green Points Program

    Users earn additional “green points” for choosing eco-friendly options (e.g., reusable packaging, opting out of cutlery), which can be redeemed for rewards or discounts.
